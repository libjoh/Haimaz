Services

Haimaz Interiors offers personalized interiors blending style, comfort, and functionality. From modular kitchens to full home designs, we tailor spaces to your taste and budget. With 3D visuals and expert execution, we bring your dream home to life-beautiful, thoughtful, and truly inspiring.

Modular Living Room

Modular living room products are pre-designed, customizable furniture and d√©cor elements that can be easily assembled, rearranged, or expanded based on the layout and needs of a space. These products include items like modular sofas, TV units, storage systems, wall-mounted shelves, and coffee tables. They are designed for flexibility, functionality, and modern aesthetics, making them ideal for urban homes where space optimization is key. The modular approach also allows homeowners to personalize their living room setup, upgrade individual components, and adapt to changing lifestyle requirements without replacing entire units.

Modular Kitchen

Modular kitchen products are factory-made, standardized kitchen components designed for efficient space utilization, functionality, and modern aesthetics. These include modules such as cabinets, drawers, shelves, pull-out units, countertops, and built-in appliances. Each unit is crafted to fit together seamlessly, allowing for easy customization based on kitchen layout (L-shaped, U-shaped, parallel, etc.). Modular kitchens offer advantages like ease of installation, maintenance, and flexibility to upgrade or reconfigure parts. They are especially popular in contemporary homes for their sleek design, organized storage solutions, and ability to maximize space in both small and large kitchens.

Dining Room Designs

A modular dining room features customizable and space-efficient furniture designed to suit various layouts and lifestyles. Key modular elements include extendable dining tables, stackable or foldable chairs, built-in storage units, sideboards, and crockery cabinets. These products are designed for flexibility, allowing easy rearrangement or expansion based on space and usage needs. Modular dining setups are ideal for modern homes, especially where space is limited, as they combine style with functionality. They also offer a cohesive look when coordinated with modular kitchen and living room designs, enhancing the overall interior aesthetic.

Bedroom Storages

Bedroom storages are essential furniture elements designed to keep the space organized and clutter-free. Common storage solutions include wardrobes, under-bed drawers, bedside tables, chest of drawers, wall-mounted shelves, and modular storage units. These storage options help maximize space by utilizing both vertical and horizontal areas effectively. Modern bedroom storages are not only functional but also enhance the room's aesthetics with sleek designs and smart features like sliding doors, built-in mirrors, and compartments for accessories. They play a key role in maintaining a clean, organized, and restful environment in the bedroom.

Pooja Room

A modular pooja room is a compact, customizable prayer unit designed to fit seamlessly into modern homes. These units are pre-designed and factory-made, offering flexibility in size, material, and layout. Modular pooja rooms can be wall-mounted, freestanding, or integrated into existing furniture like cabinets or shelves. They typically include storage for religious items, space for idols or pictures, and provisions for lighting and ventilation. Made from materials like wood, MDF, or glass, they combine traditional elements with modern design. Modular pooja rooms are ideal for small apartments and urban living, providing a sacred space without occupying much floor area.

Modern Study Units

A modular study unit is a space-efficient, customizable furniture setup designed to create a functional and organized study or work area. These units typically include a study desk, shelves, drawers, and overhead cabinets, which can be configured based on the room layout and user needs. Modular study units are ideal for homes, especially in urban settings, where space optimization is essential. They offer flexibility in design, easy installation, and a clutter-free environment for focused work or study. With a range of styles and finishes, modular study units also enhance the overall aesthetics of the room while ensuring practicality.

Interior design involves space planning, structural changes, and functionality. Decoration focuses on furnishings and aesthetics only.
2. Why should I hire an interior designer? +
An interior designer saves time, avoids costly mistakes, optimizes your space, and brings a cohesive vision to life.
3. How much does interior design cost? +
Costs vary depending on the scope of work. We offer flexible packages suited to your space and budget.

A typical home interior project takes 6-12 weeks. Timelines depend on project size, approvals, and materials.
